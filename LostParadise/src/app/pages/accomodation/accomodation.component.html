Charming bungalows embedded in nature, each with it’s own unique character and style

We offer a variety of room types to best meet your holiday needs. All of our rooms are air-conditioned and come with fresh towels and linen. We also made sure to equip each room type with it’s own little terrace in front of the room, en suite bath room and a king or queen size bed. Breakfast is always included with your stay. Starting from 8:00AM we are serving ala carte breakfast with a selection of cook to order options. The banana pancakes with cinnamon and honey or chocolate topping are among the favorites. We also offer a classic continental breakfast and a vegan option with your choice of tea or Lombok coffe.
