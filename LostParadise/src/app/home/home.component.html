Green sustainability was important to us when we built Lost Paradise. We also wanted to create something that goes in line with local traditions and architecture. The majestic Javanese Joglo was built using traditional building techniques and recycled wood from the forests of Borneo. Under its roof you will experience the beauty of open air living in the tropics. We source our ingredients locally and our staff prepares a selection of local Indonesian dishes, freshly blended fruit smoothies and cold beverages for you to enjoy in the shade of the joglo or in the loungers by the pool.
