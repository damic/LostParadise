General Lee’s Bar 49.2107581N, 16.6188150E

Is it a Colombian cocaine smugglers landing strip? Is it a mirage in the desert?

No, it’s General Lee’s Tavern serving classic cocktails and frosty Bintangs from 4 PM til late! Next to all the classics like Gin & Tonic and Cosmopolitan we also have a selection of fresh and popular rum based drinks like Caipirinha and Mojito, or try one of the house favorites. Skinny bitch (Vodka, soda, lime) and of course shaken Margharitas and Lee’s Sunrise for all fans of tequila!
